Food Pantry Details

What the Maize and Blue Cupboard Provides

Food

This includes produce, dairy, meat, bread, frozen, and shelf-stable food (rice, canned food, tuna, etc.).

Kitchen and Cooking

Items such as dishes, pots, pans, silverware, Tupperware, cutting boards, knives, and more.

Personal and Household

Items such as trash bags, toilet paper, cleaning supplies, hygine products, school supplies, baby items, and more.

Support

Guidance towards resources such as CAPS, the Dean of Students and Financial Aid, and SNAP (Michigan's food program).
