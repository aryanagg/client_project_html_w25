Emergency Contact Information

Emergency Services: Call 911 for any emergency or safety situation.

UMich Division of Public Safety and Security (DPSS): Call DPSS at (734)-764-1131 for any non-emergency safety issues.

UMich Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS): Call the 24/7 hotline at (734)-764-8312 for urgent mental health assistance and for scheduling mental health appointments on the same day.

UMich Hospital Psychiatric Emergency Room: Call (734)-996-4747 for emergency psychiatric assistance.

UMich Sexual Assault Prevention and Awareness Center: Call the 24/7 hotline at (734)-936-3333 for support and resources related to sexual assault, sexual harrassment, and gender-based harrassment.

SafeRide Late Night Transportation: Call (734)-647-8000 for free transportation to a house or car within a mile of campus when you show your MCard.
